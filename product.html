Materials

American Black Walnut
Solid Juglans nigra, air-dried 18 months. Panels book-matched for continuous grain. Finished with penetrating Danish oil — a matte surface that deepens richly with age.
Tempered Float Glass
8mm tempered safety glass, low-iron clarity, polished flat edges. Precision-routed walnut rebate with silicone isolation gaskets to eliminate rattle entirely.
Charcoal Wool Boucle
Premium wool-blend boucle over high-resilience foam. Loop weave provides grip and warmth. Removable cover for care. Available in three tonal options including ivory and umber.
